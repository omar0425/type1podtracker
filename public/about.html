Our story

Hi — I'm a parent of a kid with Type 1 diabetes. My daughter Brooklyn lives with T1D every single day, and like every T1D family we know, we've had our share of sensors that quit on day 4 and pods that screeched to a halt in the middle of the night.

Every one of those failed devices is replaceable for free — Dexcom and Insulet both stand behind their products. But only if you can find the serial number, the lot number, the dates… and that box went in the trash a week ago. We were losing replacements we were owed, simply because we didn't have the paperwork.

So I built this tracker: a 30-second habit at every site change that means we never lose that information again. It's free, it doesn't collect anything, and your family's data never leaves your own computer. If it saves your family even one $80 sensor, it did its job.

If you use a different pump or CGM and want it added, send me a note — I read every message.

— Brooklyn's parent, and the whole team behind Poddy 🫂
