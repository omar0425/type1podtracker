What it does

  • 30-second logging at every sensor or pod change — serial, lot & sequence numbers saved before the box hits the trash.
  • Live wear countdowns — "Day 4 of 10" for sensors, "Day 2 of 3" for pods, with change-by dates.
  • One-tap failure reports — record what happened while it's fresh, with the exact info Dexcom and Insulet ask for.
  • Printable claim forms — a clean one-page record per failed device, ready to reference when you call or go online.
  • Color-coded Excel exports — failures highlighted, filter by date range, perfect for insurance and supply counts.
  • Private by design — everything stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no data collection. Built-in backup & restore, plus optional auto-save to a real file on your computer that browser data-clearing can't touch (Chrome/Edge).

⬇ Install it like an app

The tracker installs straight from your browser onto Windows, Mac, and Chromebooks — it gets its own icon, opens in its own window, and works offline. No download files, no updates to manage: it's always the latest version.

💻 Windows

  1. Open the app in Chrome or Edge
  2. Click the install icon in the address bar (a little screen with a ↓)
  3. Click Install — it's now in your Start menu

 Mac

  1. Open the app in Safari or Chrome
  2. Safari: File → Add to Dock
    Chrome: install icon in the address bar
  3. Launch it from your Dock like any app

💾 Chromebook

  1. Open the app in Chrome
  2. Click the install icon in the address bar
  3. It appears in your launcher & shelf

Your log is saved on the device you use it on — use Save backup file inside the app to move it between computers or keep a safety copy. The app's "stop your browser from deleting the log" tips (in the Backup section) show how to keep your records safe when clearing browsing data. On iPhone/iPad, installing to the Home Screen gives the app its own storage that Safari's data-clearing doesn't touch.
